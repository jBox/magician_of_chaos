Magician of Chaos

Light and Dark combine to create the ultimate Magician! Witness the power of Chaos!

"Magician of Chaos" - This monster destroys 1 card on the field when the effect of a Spell or Trap Card is activated. Use it with Equip Spell Cards or Continuous Trap Cards that can be activated every turn. In addition, even if this monster is destroyed, you can Special Summon a "Chaos" or "Black Luster Soldier" Ritual Monster from your hand.
